About This Tool

This tool automatically fetches analytics data from Sprout Social and updates Google Sheets with formatted data for different social media platforms including:

  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • LinkedIn
  • Twitter
  • YouTube

The data is organized into separate sheets for each platform with proper formatting and calculations.
